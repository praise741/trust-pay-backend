🚀 Get Started:

For Sellers:

  • Create payment links for your products
  • Share links with buyers on Instagram, WhatsApp, etc.
  • Get paid securely after delivery confirmation

For Buyers:

  • Pay securely with funds held in escrow
  • Confirm delivery when you receive your item
  • Open disputes if there's an issue

💡 How It Works:

  1. Seller creates a deal and shares the payment link
  2. Buyer pays - funds are held securely
  3. Seller ships the item
  4. Buyer confirms delivery
  5. Funds are released to seller
